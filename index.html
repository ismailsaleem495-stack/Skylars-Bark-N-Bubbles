03 About Katie

Calm, patient,
positive grooming.

I work from a quiet home environment in Crawley, offering one-on-one appointments so your dog has my full attention. No busy salon noises, no cages, no other dogs — just a calm, peaceful space designed to help your dog feel secure.

I always go at your dog's pace, reading their body language and watching for signs of stress. If they need a pause, we take a break; if they need reassurance, we slow down. Every appointment begins and ends with a short enrichment activity, so your dog leaves feeling happy and confident.

This approach is especially helpful for nervous or sensitive dogs, puppies new to grooming, and dogs who've had difficult past experiences.
